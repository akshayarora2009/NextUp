Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is NextUp and how can I use it?
NextUp is an instant time table app that quickly helps you to figure out the subject and lecture hall details for each working day. Its a user-friendly app that requires you to only select your credentials such as branch, year etc. and figure out the required time table.

Q2. Does the app give me my latest time table with all requisite changes?
Team NextUp is committed to provide you with the latest time tables incoporating all changes. As soon as any deviations are reported/observed we update it.You should regularly check for app updates on the playstore.

Q3.Can I change the time table on my own?
You don't have to as we are just a click away! Any changes you wish to see in the time table can be conveyed to us at developer.nextup@gmail.com

Q4.The navigation bar is broken. What do i do?
You just require internet connectivity and have to press the refresh button.
